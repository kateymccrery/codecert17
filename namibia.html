Namibia seems like an amazing place in terms of food, geography, and cultural way of life.

  • Outdoor cooking is part of the Namibian way of life. The traditional braaivleis (meat barbeque) is a tasty meal, as is potjiekos, a spicy stew of meat, chicken and fish cooked in a cast iron, three-legged pot over an open fire. Click here to see what the typical Namibian cuisine is.

  • Namibia is one of the places where the ocean meets the desert. This is the landscape referenced above, and is on my bucket list of places to see before I die. The Namib Desert is a coastal desert, which you can find more about here.

  • The capital of Namibia is Windhoek, which is roughly about the size of New Orleans at 325,858 in 2011. Click here to find out more about the capital.
